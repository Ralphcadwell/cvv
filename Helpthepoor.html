Little Dreams Home

The society is not perfect, and we have people who need our help in one way or the other.

Summary

Little Dreams Home is part of a global network of non-profit organizations leading the worldwide movement to end the need for orphanages in our lifetime It is a home for children who have been Abused, Abandoned; Orphans who are on the street struggling Alone. Our goal is to provide hope for these children so they can attain a brighter future and a vision of life beyond their present vulnerable situations.

Challange

The number of orphans and vulnerable children in the world in 2021 is estimated at 153 million, out of which 16.5 million children worldwide have lost their mother, father or both parents due to HIV / AIDS . Some of these children are left with their poor grandparents, some of the children are with mentally ill mothers, sleeping under the bridges, while others roam on streets, exposing them to vulnerable situations of either being sexual exploited at a young and tender age or being sold into child labor. Majority of these children are denied basic rights. About 1.7 million childrenhave HIV infection. Each year, about 160,000 more children are infected and about 100,000 children die. Children in orphanage resident care face many difficulties such as poverty, poor physical health, attachment disorders, inadequate social skills, and mental health difficulties. Added to this is the loss of one or both parents, which is difficult for the children.

Long Term Impact

We strongly believe that with proper intervention, these children will thrive, develop to their full potential, and positively influence and contribute to their immediate communities and to the society at large. It will create awareness to the community and the outside world for the need to protect, promote, preserve and support the vulnerable children and the orphans including their rights.This project will provide a safe and permanent home for the children. It will reduce the suffering of the orphans and vulnerable children, It will build their capacity through giving them love, care, good food education and simple activities to do so. It will provide adequate education to make them good citizens and have a better future through good education right from a tender age.

The world and global society benefit when we help orphan children. Instead of harming themselves and others, they grow up to be responsible, healthy, adults that give back to the community they live in instead of taking away from it.
